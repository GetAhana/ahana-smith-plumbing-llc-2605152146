How we handle Water heaters

Water heater replacement is straightforward when you know the steps.

  • Call for a free estimate — Tell us your current heater type and size. We quote the job with no obligation.
  • Schedule the swap — Pick a time that works. Most replacements take half a day. We shut down the old unit safely and disconnect.
  • Install and test — We install the new unit, hook up the gas or electric, test for leaks, and haul the old tank away.
When to call us

Replace your water heater if you notice these signs.

  • No hot water or water runs out after a few minutes.
  • Rusty water coming from the tap or visible rust on the tank.
  • Loud rumbling or popping noises inside the tank.
  • Water pooling around the base of the tank.
  • Unit is 10 years old or older and losing temperature.

FAQ — Water heaters

How long does a water heater replacement take?

Most replacements are done in two to four hours, including removal of the old unit and disposal. We try to minimize your downtime.

Do you install tankless water heaters?

Yes, we install both tank and tankless models. Tankless units take longer but save space and run more efficiently. Ask about the trade-offs during your estimate.

What warranty comes with a new heater?

We warranty our labor for one year. The unit itself usually carries a manufacturer warranty—we provide the details when we install.
